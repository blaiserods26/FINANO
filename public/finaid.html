Financial Aid and Scholarships

Eligibility Criteria

The applicant must be a domicile of Maharashtra.
They must have scored at least 60% marks in Classes 10 and 12.
The annual family income of the applicant must be between INR 2 Lakh to INR 8 Lakh (depending on the scholarship).

Documents Required

Scanned photo and signature
Aadhar Card
School/college leaving certificate
Cap allotment letter
College fee receipt
Marksheet of Class 10
Marksheet of Class 12
Previous Year Marksheet
All semester marksheets
Caste certificate
Income certificate of current financial year (from Tehsildar)
Bank passbook
Self declaration certificate
Certificate of domicile
Ration card
Hostel certificate (if required)
Gap certificate (if required)
Handicap certificate (if required)

Indian Government Scholarships

The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is an online portal by the Government of India for applying, processing, verifying and sanction of Government scholarships to students.

Maharashtra Government Scholarships

Mahadbt portal provides the benefits of many social welfare schemes like e-Scholarships to the students of Maharashtra. The primary objective of the Aaple Sarkar DBT portal 2023 is to assure the effective disbursement and implementation of scholarships within Maharashtra

National Government Service Portal

Students can register online on the National Government Service Portal to search and apply for educational scholarships. You can register, fill up a single form and apply to multiple banks for educational loans.
